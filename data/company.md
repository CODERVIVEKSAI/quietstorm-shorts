# Golden Lady

**Tagline:** A Step Towards Healthy Living

**What we make:** Naturally pounded spices and cold-pressed oils.

**Why we're different:**
- Spices are **hand-pounded** (stone mortar, traditional method) — preserves aromatic oils that machine-grinding destroys with heat
- Oils are **cold-pressed** — no chemical solvents, no heat-damaged fatty acids, higher nutrient retention
- Small-batch, traceable sourcing
- Rooted in traditional Indian kitchen wisdom, made for modern health-conscious households

**Target audience:** Indian families (and the diaspora) who cook at home, care about ingredient quality, and want to move away from mass-produced supermarket spices and refined oils.

**Tone for ads:**
- Warm, trustworthy, grandmother-kitchen feel
- NO hype, yelling, or "BUY NOW!!!" energy
- Educate > sell. One clear benefit per ad.
- Okay to reference tradition, health, taste, family

**Products to rotate through (one per ad, cycle weekly):**
- Turmeric powder (immunity, curcumin)
- Red chili powder (flavor depth, no synthetic color)
- Coriander powder (digestion)
- Cumin powder (metabolism)
- Black pepper (absorption booster)
- Garam masala (signature blend)
- Sesame (gingelly) oil — cold pressed
- Coconut oil — cold pressed
- Groundnut (peanut) oil — cold pressed
- Mustard oil — cold pressed

**CTA options (rotate):**
- "Link in bio to order."
- "DM us for bulk orders."
- "Available on [website/platform] — link in bio."
- "Follow for more on traditional Indian kitchen wisdom."

**Things NOT to say:**
- Medical claims ("cures X disease")
- Attacks on competitors by name
- "Chemical-free" (technically everything is chemicals — say "no synthetic additives" instead)
